

  ## 1.Preparation

Before using the IDAGIO music service, you need to prepare a valid IDAGIO account.
 
For first-time users, open IDAGIO on your device under "Streaming" - "Music", or open it in the Eversolo Control app under "Discover" - "Music Services". Follow the on-screen prompts to complete login.

  ## 2.Log In

Please enter your IDAGIO account and password on your device or Eversolo Control APP to log in.
 
If you haven't created a IDAGIO account yet, please register on the IDAGIO official website: https://app.idagio.com/discover , or on the IDAGIO official app.

## 3.Browsing

After successfully logging in, you can browse IDAGIO music on your device or the Eversolo Control APP. At the top of the interface, you will see sections for Discover, Explore, Mood, Recently Played, Collection.
 
You can browse these categories layer by layer to access various IDAGIO music recommendations. In Collection section, you can also view your saved tracks, works, playlists, albums, tracks, artists.

## 5.More


In the browsing interface, you can access the more functions menu by clicking the "<img class="imgIcon" src="/icons/qqMusic_1.png" alt="" />" icon to the right of the track, or the "<img class="imgIcon" src="/icons/qqMusic_2.png" alt="" />" icon in the upper right corner of the album/playlist.


This menu supports the following functions:


| More menus for singles | More menus for albums/playlists |
| :--- | :--- |
| Play now / Play next | Play all tracks now |
| Add to the end of play queue | Add all tracks to the next play |
| Add to device playlist | Add all tracks to the end of play queue |
| Favorite to IDAGIO | Add to device playlist |
| Add to playlist | Favorite to IDAGIO |
| /  | Add to IDAGIO playlist |


**Tips:**
+ Saved content can be viewed and managed at any time in the "Collection" section.
+ Content added to the device playlist can be viewed and managed centrally in My Favorites- Device playlists.

## 6.Streaming Quality


When playing IDAGIO music, you can click the '<img class="imgIcon" src="/icons/idagio_1.png" alt="" />' icon in the top right corner of the homepage, or click the '<img class="imgIcon" src="/icons/qqMusic_3.png" alt="" />' icon on the playback details interface to select the streaming quality mode.
 
Supported options include:

+ Low
+ Medium（128 kb/s）
+ Hight（320 kb/s）
+ Lossless（CD-Quality）
+ HiRes（Master）
