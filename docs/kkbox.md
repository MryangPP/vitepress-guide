

  ## 1. Preparation


Before using the KKBOX music service, you need to prepare a valid KKBOX account.
 
For first-time users, please open KKBOX in the "Streaming" - "Music" menu on your device and follow the prompts to log in.

  ## 2. Log In


Please enter your KKBOX account and password on your device to log in.
 
If you haven't created a KKBOX account yet, you can scroll to the bottom of the page, click "Sign up," and follow the prompts to complete account registration.





<img src="/imagesEN/kkbox_03.png" />

## 3. Browsing


After successfully logging in, you can browse KKBOX music on your device or the Eversolo Control APP. At the top of the interface, you will see sections for Found,Charts,Genre&Mood, Radio, and My.

You can browse these categories layer by layer to access various KKBOX music recommendations. You can also view your saved tracks, albums, playlists, and user-created playlists in the My section.

## 4. Search


Click the search box at the top to search the entire KKBOX music library for tracks, albums, playlists, artists, and other content you want, quickly locating your desired music resources.

## 5. More

In the browsing interface, you can access the more functions menu by clicking the "<img class="imgIcon" src="/icons/qqMusic_1.png" alt="" />" icon to the right of the track, or the "<img class="imgIcon" src="/icons/qqMusic_2.png" alt="" />" icon in the upper right corner of the album/playlist.

This menu supports the following functionsï¼š

| More menus for singles | More menus for albums/playlists |
| :--- | :--- |
| Play now / Play next | Play all tracks now |
| Add to the end of play queue | Add all tracks to the next play |
| Add to device playlist | Add all tracks to the end of play queue |
| Save to KKBOX | Add to device playlist |
| Add to my album collection | / |
| Add to playlist | / |


**Tips:**
+ Saved content can be viewed and managed at any time in the "My" section.
+ Content added to the device playlist can be viewed and managed centrally in My Music- Device playlists.

## 6. Streaming Quality

When playing KKBOX music, enter the playback details interface and click the "<img class="imgIcon" src="/icons/qqMusic_3.png" alt="" />" icon at the left top to select the stream quality mode. Supported options include:

+ 320k
+ 16-bit CD quality
+ 24-bit Hi-Res quality


> Note: Not all songs support 24-bit Hi-Res; the actual usable stream quality depends on the original file quality of the music.
