

  ## 1. Preparation


Before using the Presto Music service, you need to prepare a valid Presto Music account.
 
For first-time users, open Presto Music on your device under "Streaming" - "Music", or open it in the Eversolo Control app under "Discover" - "Music Services". Follow the on-screen prompts to complete login.

  ## 2. Log In

Please enter your Presto Music account and password on your device or Eversolo Control APP to log in.
 
If you haven't created a Presto Music account yet, please click ''Start your free trial today'' and follow the prompts to complete account registration.

## 3. Browsing

After successfully logging in, you can browse Presto Music on your device or the Eversolo Control APP. At the top of the interface, you will see sections for HOME, EXPLORE, COLLECTION.
 
You can browse these categories layer by layer to access various Presto Music recommendations. In COLLECTION section, you can also view your personal data in your Presto Music account, including albums, playlist, composers, artists and others that you've saved on the official Presto Music platform.

## 5. More


In the browsing interface, you can access the more functions menu by clicking the "<img class="imgIcon" src="/icons/qqMusic_1.png" alt="" />" icon to the right of the track, or the "<img class="imgIcon" src="/icons/qqMusic_2.png" alt="" />" icon in the upper right corner of the album/playlist.


This menu supports the following functions:


| More menus for singles | More menus for albums/playlists |
| :--- | :--- |
| Add to device playlist | Play all tracks now |
| / | Add all tracks to the next play |
| / | Add all tracks to the end of play queue |
| / | Add to device playlist |



**Tips:**
+  Content added to the device playlist can be viewed and managed centrally in My Music- Device playlists.

## 6. Streaming Quality

When playing Presto Music, the device outputs directly according to the specifications of the current audio source, and the streaming quality entirely depends on the original files on this platform.




Supported options include:

+ Lossless 16bit 44.1kHz
+ HiRes 24bit 96~192kHz
