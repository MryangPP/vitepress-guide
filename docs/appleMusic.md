

  ## 1. Preparation

Before using Apple Music, please ensure you have the official Apple Music app installed and are logged in with your Apple ID.

For first-time users, please open Apple Music in the "Streaming" - "Music" menu on your device and follow the prompts to authorize your account in the official app.






<img src="/imagesEN/appleMusic_01.png" />

  ## 2. Log In


Please click "Start Authorization" and enter your Apple ID password as prompted to complete the authorization.
 
If you haven't created an Apple ID yet, please go to the official Apple Music app to register an account.







<img src="/imagesEN/appleMusic_02.png" />

## 3. Browsing



After successfully logging in, you can browse Apple Music on your device or the Eversolo Control app. At the top of the interface, you will see sections for Home, Charts, and Library.
 
You can browse these categories layer by layer, access various recommended content from Apple Music Selection, and view your saved playlists, albums, songs, and artists in the Library section.

## 5. More


In the browsing interface, you can access the more options menu by clicking the "<img class="imgIcon" src="/icons/qqMusic_1.png" alt="" />" icon to the right of a track or the "<img class="imgIcon" src="/icons/qqMusic_2.png" alt="" />" icon in the upper right corner of an album/playlist.


This menu supports the following functions:


| More menus for singles | More menus for albums/playlists |
| :--- | :--- |
| Play now / Play next | Play all tracks now |
| Add to the end of play queue | Add all tracks to play next |
| Add to device playlist | Add all tracks to the end of the play queue |
| Add to Library | Add to device playlist |
| / | Add to library |



**Tips:**

+ Saved content can be viewed and managed at any time in the "Library" section.
+ Content added to your device's playlists can be viewed and managed centrally in My Music - Device Playlists.

## 6. Streaming Quality


Due to adjustments in the service provider's user policies or interface permissions, the integrated Apple Music service only supports 44.1kHz audio quality.
